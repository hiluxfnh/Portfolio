About Me

Hilux Fokou Ngoumo

Hello! I'm Hilux, a passionate advocate for change, technology enthusiast, and aspiring leader. Hailing from Cameroon, I've always been driven by the desire to address the challenges faced by my community and beyond. Pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from GITAM DEEMED University, my journey has been a blend of innovation and impact. As a Front End Web Developer Lead at CODEX, I led team of passionate students into web Development projects and Workshops.

My commitment to social progress extends to being a young person co-chair at Inter-Agency Working Group(IAWG) SWG on Reproductive Health, where I advocated for youth-inclusive policies on an international stage focusing on Adolescent Sexual Reproductive Health in Humanitarian Settings.
Inspired by my community, I founded 'KamerLark', a pioneering platform addressing student housing challenges in Cameroon. This venture marries my tech-savviness with social impact, underlining my dedication to improving lives through innovation. My journey is a testament to my belief that innovation, leadership, and advocacy are vital tools for transforming our world. Join me as I continue my pursuit of meaningful change and the fusion of technology with social good.

Projects

KamerLark - Student Housing Platform

KamerLark is a pioneering platform created to revolutionize student housing in Cameroon. It's the first of its kind, eliminating the challenges students face while searching for accommodation. Through KamerLark, students can easily find suitable housing options, while property owners effortlessly showcase their properties. With a team of skilled individuals, including experts in Audit Accounting and Cloud Security, we're on a mission to impact over 200 million students in Africa by 2035.
